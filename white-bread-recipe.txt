Ingredients:
 - 1 1/2 cups water, room temp
 - 3 teaspoons unsalted butter, room temp, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
 - 1 teaspoon granulated sugar or honey
 - 1 1/2 teaspoonss salt
 - 4 cups bread flour
 - 1/4 cup nonfat dry milk
 - 1 1/2 teaspoons yeast, active dry,instant or bread machine

Directions:
 1. Place all ingredients , in the order listed, in the bread pan fitted with
    the kneading paddle.  Secure the bread pan in the Cuisinart Automatic Bread Maker.

 2. Press the menu button to select the Basic/White program.  Press loaf size and crust
    color buttons to select both size  and crust preference.  Press start/Stop to mix, knead,
    rise and bake.

 3. When the cycle is complete, remove the bread pan from the machine and transfer bread to
    wire rack to cool completely before slicing.
